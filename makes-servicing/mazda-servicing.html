Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Mazda manufacturer service cost?

You could make big savings by having your Mazda serviced with us compared to a main dealer. We will calculate the cost for you upon request. Please let us know if you would like us to use official Mazda parts or equivalent parts.

Can you update my Mazda online service history?

Yes, we are registered as a independent operator with Mazda and can view and update your online service record. If we service your vehicle we can print this off for you on request.

I have a Mazda service plan, can you service my car?

A Mazda service plan usually means that servicing at a Mazda main dealer is covered within your plan. Currently, we cannot undertake service plan work. However, we can still carry out other work such as MOT, Aircon and brake services.

Value Service

Full Service

A full value service with us includes:

  • Oil change
  • Oil filter
  • Pollen filter
  • Air filter
  • Spark plugs*
  • Fuel filter
