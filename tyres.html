Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check the size of my tyres?

The size of a tyre is written on the sidewall. It consists of numbers and letters and will look like:
205 - Width of the tyre in millimetres.
55 - Height of the tyre sidewall as a percentage of the width.
R17 - The diameter of the tyre's inner rim in inches.
91 - Load Rating of the tyre.
V - Speed Rating. Indicates the maximum speed for the tyre when at full load.

What is the legal tread depth limit in the UK for cars?

The legal minimum tread depth for car tyres in the United Kingdom is 1.6 mm, across the central 75% of the tread around the complete circumference of the tyre.
